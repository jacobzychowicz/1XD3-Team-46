Client Information

Name: Zak Yarwood

Organizational information: N/A

Client Q/A

1. Do you have a preference on what the project name should be or can we just come up with it?
"I have no preference, you can come up with it. Preferably something more professional."

2. Who are the target users of the app?
"Typically people who are in high school or undergrad who are interested in health sciences or medecine. Most people will probably be on desktop rather than mobile."

3. What static information should be available through the app?
"The users should be able to see my mission statement, as well as an about me and contact links. There should also be a disclaimer that the website is for educational purposes only, and is in no way medical advice."

4. What kind of design do you want?
"It should have a navbar, and I like the design of the McMaster Web of Science website. I want it to look professional and clean rather than cluttered."

5. What data needs to be managed and presented to the user?
"There should be categorized content like my research on neuroscience, fitness and health, and in the future cardiovascular. It should include a timeline for when I did each piece of research. Under each article there should be an area where the user is able to comment on his research. I should be able to edit the articles and the user should only be able to comment."

6. Any interactive elements?
"There should be hover animations, including when you hover over an image you get a little bit of information about the image. Maybe there could be a little flashcard area at some point in the articles to have the user able to study the content."
